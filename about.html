About WonderPods

Who We Are

WonderPods is a family-run business passionate about isopods, springtails, and creating thriving ecosystems. Based in Missoula, Montana, we take pride in providing high-quality isopods and springtails to hobbyists and reptile enthusiasts across the state.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable and ethical husbandry practices while sharing the joy of isopod keeping. We strive to educate our customers about these fascinating critters and the role they play in both the natural and more importantly, your created environments.

Why Choose Us?

At WonderPods, we prioritize quality and customer satisfaction. Each of our isopods and springtails is hand-selected and packaged with care to ensure they transport healthy and are ready to thrive in your setup. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced keeper, we're here to help with expert advice and support.
